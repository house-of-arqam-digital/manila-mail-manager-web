Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Easy unsubscribe

Click unsubscribe and Manila Mail opens the right page for you. No more hunting for tiny links buried in email footers.

Automatic detection

New subscriptions get tagged the moment they arrive — no re-scanning. Always on, and entirely in your browser.

Bulk cleanup

Select several subscriptions and open their unsubscribe pages together, instead of hunting them down one at a time.

Insights engine

See which subscriptions email you most, spot dormant ones and weekly trends, and get one-tap cleanup suggestions.

Inbox health score

A single glanceable grade for how managed your subscriptions are — with a breakdown of what's dragging it down and the top fix to raise it.

Subscription spend tracker

See every paid service emailing your inbox and track your monthly and yearly spend. Prices are auto-detected from invoices you open — no manual tallying.

Gmail badges

Subscription emails are tagged right in your Gmail inbox so you can spot them instantly without opening.

Private by design

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your email data is never sent to any external server.
