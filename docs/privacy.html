Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

The short version

Manila Mail Manager operates no servers. All processing happens in your browser. Your email contents are never sent to us or anyone else — they never leave your device.

What we collect

Subscription detection runs locally in your browser as you use Gmail. We do not collect, store, or transmit your email content to any external server.

Data that stays on your device

  • Detected subscription sender addresses
  • Email counts per sender
  • Your extension settings and preferences
  • Unsubscribe history
  • Locally-computed analytics snapshots

All of this data is stored in Chrome's local storage on your device. We have no servers, so there is nowhere for it to be sent.

Unsubscribe requests

When you unsubscribe from a sender, your browser sends an HTTP request directly to the sender's unsubscribe URL (typically following RFC 8058). These requests do not pass through any Manila Mail Manager server because no such server exists.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Paddle. We do not store credit card numbers or billing details. Paddle handles all payment processing under their own privacy policy.

Analytics and telemetry

Manila Mail Manager does not collect any usage analytics or telemetry. We have no visibility into how you use the extension.

Third-party services

  • Paddle — for payment processing (if you subscribe to Pro)

Data deletion and your rights

You can delete all stored data at any time from Settings > Data > Clear All Data. Uninstalling the extension also removes all local data.

Under GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and similar privacy laws, you have rights to access, correct, export, and delete your personal data. Because all your data is already on your own device, you can exercise these rights directly: open the extension's storage in Chrome's developer tools to view it, or clear it via Settings or uninstall. We don't hold any data about you separately.

Children

Manila Mail Manager is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
